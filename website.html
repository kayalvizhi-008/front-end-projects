Travel, broadly defined, is the movement of people between different locations, which can encompass various forms like commuting, business travel, or vacationing. It involves using different modes of transportation, including foot, bicycle, cars, trains, buses, ships, and airplanes, and can be for a short period or extended stays.
Travel can also be part of tourism, with people staying overnight in hotels, hostels, or other accommodations.
